myMoney widget

The widget makes it more easy for you to track your budget on the go. It allows you to add a new entry with a single tap.

To activate it, open the Notification-Center (swipe down from the top of your iPhone) > Swipe to the left > Scroll down to "Edit" > Activate "myMoney" and put it to the top of the list

Personal categories

Even if myMoney tracks your incomes and spendings based on a budget, it allows you to define your own categories and gives you the possibility to decide whether it should count on the budget or not.

Tip: Not budget relevant categories are perfect for special spendings or periodic incomes/spendings like salary or rental fee.
